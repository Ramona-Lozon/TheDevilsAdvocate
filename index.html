The Devils Advocate

The Devil’s Advocate is an accounting firm built on a simple but uncompromising principle: every number deserves scrutiny, and every decision deserves a second look. Founded and led by Zoe Beecham, The Devil’s Advocate exists to challenge assumptions, identify risk before it becomes costly, and ensure that financial strategies stand up under pressure. Where others may accept surface-level answers, we ask the harder questions—because strong finances are forged through rigor, not convenience. Zoe brings a sharp analytical approach and a deep understanding of accounting, compliance, and financial strategy. Her leadership sets the tone for the firm: precise, ethical, and relentlessly thorough. Clients trust The Devil’s Advocate not just to prepare their books, but to protect their interests by anticipating problems, stress-testing decisions, and advocating for the most defensible financial outcomes. We work with individuals and businesses who value clarity, accountability, and long-term stability. Whether navigating complex tax matters, ensuring regulatory compliance, or building a sound financial foundation, we act as a critical partner—one who is unafraid to challenge, question, and ultimately strengthen your position. At The Devil’s Advocate, we believe that the best financial advice is not the easiest to hear, but the most honest.
